Overview

ERADAT is a real estate investment company that was incorporated in 2006. It was later restructured as a Kuwait Shareholding Real Estate Company in 2010. Its current paid-up capital is KD 13.2 million and operates in the State of Kuwait. The company possesses total assets amounting KD 54.4 million. Its major shareholders are Kuwait Real Estate Company KPSC (AQARAT) and Sanam Real Estate Company KPSC.
