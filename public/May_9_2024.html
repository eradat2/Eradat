Eradat International Holding Company Convenes Ordinary General Assembly Meeting for 2024

Eradat International Holding Company held its Ordinary General Assembly Meeting at 12:00 PM on Thursday, May 9, 2024, at the company’s headquarters. The meeting was chaired by Dr. Sulaiman Tariq Al-Mutairi, with the attendance of shareholders and company auditors from Abdul Latif Al-Aiban Office and Grant Thornton.

The General Assembly reviewed and approved the Board of Directors’ report, external auditor’s report, and financial statements for the fiscal year ending 31/12/2023. It also approved the Corporate Governance report, related party transactions, and confirmed there were no regulatory violations or financial penalties in 2023.

The shareholders approved the Board’s recommendation not to distribute dividends for the fiscal year and approved the discharge of liability of the Board members for the same period. Additionally, the appointment of external auditors and the Shariah Supervisory Board for the fiscal year ending 31/12/2024 was confirmed.

The meeting concluded at 12:15 PM.
